why Ireland?

Study Abroad in Ireland with Yaadhum OverseasEducation

Discover the world while studying abroad in Ireland!
Study abroad in Ireland with CEA and experience the country’s renowned literary, artistic, and musical heritage. Choose from study abroad programs in the capital city of Dublin or the university town of Galway, both known as centers of Irish arts and culture. Catch a glimpse of traditional Irish life while you earn credit studying abroad in Ireland for a summer, semester, or academic year.
